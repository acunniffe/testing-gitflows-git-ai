# Denis Villeneuve Filmography

## Feature Films as Director

**Un 32 août sur terre** (August 32nd on Earth) (1998)
- Directorial debut
- Stars: Pascale Bussières, Alexis Martin

**Maelström** (2000)
- Stars: Marie-Josée Croze, Jean-Nicolas Verreault

**Polytechnique** (2009)
- Based on the 1989 École Polytechnique massacre
- Stars: Maxim Gaudette, Sébastien Huberdeau, Karine Vanasse

**Incendies** (2010)
- Academy Award nominee for Best Foreign Language Film
- Stars: Lubna Azabal, Mélissa Désormeaux-Poulin, Maxim Gaudette

**Prisoners** (2013)
- English-language debut
- Stars: Hugh Jackman, Jake Gyllenhaal, Viola Davis, Maria Bello, Terrence Howard, Melissa Leo, Paul Dano

**Enemy** (2013)
- Stars: Jake Gyllenhaal, Mélanie Laurent, Sarah Gadon

**Sicario** (2015)
- Stars: Emily Blunt, Benicio del Toro, Josh Brolin

**Arrival** (2016)
- Based on Ted Chiang's "Story of Your Life"
- Stars: Amy Adams, Jeremy Renner, Forest Whitaker

**Blade Runner 2049** (2017)
- Sequel to Blade Runner (1982)
- Stars: Ryan Gosling, Harrison Ford, Ana de Armas, Sylvia Hoeks, Robin Wright, Mackenzie Davis, Carla Juri, Lennie James, Dave Bautista, Jared Leto

**Dune** (2021)
- Based on Frank Herbert's novel
- Stars: Timothée Chalamet, Rebecca Ferguson, Oscar Isaac, Josh Brolin, Stellan Skarsgård, Dave Bautista, Stephen McKinley Henderson, Zendaya, David Dastmalchian, Chang Chen, Sharon Duncan-Brewster, Charlotte Rampling, Jason Momoa, Javier Bardem

**Dune: Part Two** (2024)
- Based on Frank Herbert's novel
- Stars: Timothée Chalamet, Zendaya, Rebecca Ferguson, Josh Brolin, Austin Butler, Florence Pugh, Dave Bautista, Christopher Walken, Léa Seydoux, Javier Bardem, Stellan Skarsgård, Stephen McKinley Henderson
