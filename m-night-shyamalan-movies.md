# M. Night Shyamalan Filmography

## Feature Films as Director

**Praying with Anger** (1992)
- Directorial debut
- Stars: M. Night Shyamalan

**Wide Awake** (1998)
- Stars: Joseph Cross, Timothy Reifsnyder, Dana Delany, Denis Leary, Robert Loggia

**The Sixth Sense** (1999)
- Stars: Bruce Willis, Haley Joel Osment, Toni Collette, Olivia Williams

**Unbreakable** (2000)
- Stars: Bruce Willis, Samuel L. Jackson, Robin Wright

**Signs** (2002)
- Stars: Mel Gibson, Joaquin Phoenix, Rory Culkin, Abigail Breslin

**The Village** (2004)
- Stars: Bryce Dallas Howard, Joaquin Phoenix, Adrien Brody, William Hurt, Sigourney Weaver

**Lady in the Water** (2006)
- Stars: Paul Giamatti, Bryce Dallas Howard

**The Happening** (2008)
- Stars: Mark Wahlberg, Zooey Deschanel, John Leguizamo

**The Last Airbender** (2010)
- Based on the animated series
- Stars: Noah Ringer, Nicola Peltz, Jackson Rathbone, Dev Patel

**Devil** (2010)
- Producer only
- Stars: Chris Messina, Caroline Dhavernas, Bokeem Woodbine

**After Earth** (2013)
- Stars: Will Smith, Jaden Smith

**The Visit** (2015)
- Found footage horror
- Stars: Olivia DeJonge, Ed Oxenbould, Deanna Dunagan, Peter McRobbie

**Split** (2016)
- Stars: James McAvoy, Anya Taylor-Joy, Betty Buckley

**Glass** (2019)
- Sequel to Unbreakable and Split
- Stars: Bruce Willis, James McAvoy, Samuel L. Jackson, Sarah Paulson

**Old** (2021)
- Stars: Gael García Bernal, Vicky Krieps, Alex Wolff, Thomasin McKenzie

**Knock at the Cabin** (2023)
- Stars: Dave Bautista, Jonathan Groff, Ben Aldridge, Nikki Amuka-Bird

**Trap** (2024)
- Stars: Josh Hartnett, Ariel Donoghue, Saleka Shyamalan, Hayley Mills

## Television Series

**Servant** (2019-2023)
- Creator and executive producer
- Stars: Lauren Ambrose, Toby Jones, Nell Tiger Free, Rupert Grint
