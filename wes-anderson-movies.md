# Wes Anderson Filmography

## Feature Films

**Bottle Rocket** (1996)
- Directorial debut
- Stars: Owen Wilson, Luke Wilson, Robert Musgrave

**Rushmore** (1998)
- Stars: Jason Schwartzman, Bill Murray, Olivia Williams

**The Royal Tenenbaums** (2001)
- Stars: Gene Hackman, Anjelica Huston, Ben Stiller, Gwyneth Paltrow, Luke Wilson, Owen Wilson, Danny Glover, Bill Murray

**The Life Aquatic with Steve Zissou** (2004)
- Stars: Bill Murray, Owen Wilson, Cate Blanchett, Anjelica Huston, Willem Dafoe

**The Darjeeling Limited** (2007)
- Stars: Owen Wilson, Adrien Brody, Jason Schwartzman

**Fantastic Mr. Fox** (2009)
- Stop-motion animated film
- Voices: George Clooney, Meryl Streep, Jason Schwartzman, Bill Murray

**Moonrise Kingdom** (2012)
- Stars: Bruce Willis, Edward Norton, Bill Murray, Frances McDormand, Tilda Swinton

**The Grand Budapest Hotel** (2014)
- Stars: Ralph Fiennes, Tony Revolori, Saoirse Ronan, Adrien Brody, Willem Dafoe

**Isle of Dogs** (2018)
- Stop-motion animated film
- Voices: Bryan Cranston, Koyu Rankin, Edward Norton, Liev Schreiber, Bill Murray

**The French Dispatch** (2021)
- Stars: Bill Murray, Benicio del Toro, Adrien Brody, Tilda Swinton, Léa Seydoux, Frances McDormand

**Asteroid City** (2023)
- Stars: Jason Schwartzman, Scarlett Johansson, Tom Hanks, Jeffrey Wright, Tilda Swinton, Bryan Cranston, Edward Norton, Adrien Brody, Liev Schreiber, Willem Dafoe, Margot Robbie, Steve Carell, Matt Dillon, Hong Chau, Maya Hawke
