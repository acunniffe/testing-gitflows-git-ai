# The Russian Canon

## 18th Century
- Mikhail Lomonosov - *Odes*
- Denis Fonvizin - *The Minor*
- Alexander Radishchev - *A Journey from St. Petersburg to Moscow*

## Early 19th Century (Golden Age of Poetry)
- Alexander Pushkin - *Eugene Onegin*, *The Captain's Daughter*, *Boris Godunov*
- Mikhail Lermontov - *A Hero of Our Time*, *The Demon*
- Nikolai Gogol - *Dead Souls*, *The Overcoat*, *The Government Inspector*

## Mid-Late 19th Century (Golden Age of the Novel)
- Ivan Turgenev - *Fathers and Sons*, *A Sportsman's Sketches*
- Fyodor Dostoevsky - *Crime and Punishment*, *The Brothers Karamazov*, *The Idiot*, *Demons*, *Notes from Underground*
- Leo Tolstoy - *War and Peace*, *Anna Karenina*, *The Death of Ivan Ilyich*
- Ivan Goncharov - *Oblomov*
- Nikolai Leskov - *Lady Macbeth of Mtsensk*
- Alexander Ostrovsky - *The Storm*
- Mikhail Saltykov-Shchedrin - *The Golovlyov Family*

## Late 19th - Early 20th Century
- Anton Chekhov - *The Cherry Orchard*, *Three Sisters*, *The Lady with the Dog*
- Maxim Gorky - *The Lower Depths*, *My Childhood*

## Silver Age of Poetry
- Alexander Blok - *The Twelve*
- Anna Akhmatova - *Requiem*
- Marina Tsvetaeva - *Collected Poems*
- Osip Mandelstam - *Stone*, *Voronezh Notebooks*
- Boris Pasternak - *Doctor Zhivago*, *My Sister, Life*

## Soviet Era
- Mikhail Bulgakov - *The Master and Margarita*, *Heart of a Dog*
- Yevgeny Zamyatin - *We*
- Isaac Babel - *Red Cavalry*
- Andrei Platonov - *The Foundation Pit*
- Mikhail Sholokhov - *And Quiet Flows the Don*
- Vasily Grossman - *Life and Fate*
- Alexander Solzhenitsyn - *The Gulag Archipelago*, *One Day in the Life of Ivan Denisovich*
- Varlam Shalamov - *Kolyma Tales*

## Late Soviet and Post-Soviet
- Joseph Brodsky - *Collected Poems*
- Andrei Tarkovsky - *Sculpting in Time*
- Venedikt Yerofeyev - *Moscow to the End of the Line*
- Lyudmila Ulitskaya - *The Funeral Party*
